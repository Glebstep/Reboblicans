Donald Drunk’s Backstory: From Visionary to Leader

Donald Drunk’s journey to leadership began with a simple observation: Bobrdonia was stagnating under weak leadership and empty promises. Born and raised in the heart of Bobrdonia, Donald witnessed firsthand the struggles of hardworking beavers and citizens who felt forgotten. He realized that the only way to save his beloved country was to take action.
